Hi! I'm Griffin Dietz.

I study children's capacities for reasoning about technology, and I design and develop systems to support children's learning about that technology. My recent work focuses on early childhood computing education, specifically around computational thinking and AI education.

I am currently a Research Scientist at Apple. Previously, I finished my PhD in Computer Science at Stanford University, where I worked with James Landay in Computer Science and Hyowon Gweon in Psychology.
