Hi! I'm Griffin Dietz.

I am a first-year PhD student studying Computer Science at Stanford University in the Human-Computer Interaction Group. I am currently working with James Landay in Computer Science and Hyowon Gweon in Psychology.

I'm interested in how we can build systems to support children's cognitive development. My current work focuses on early childhood Computer Science Education, specifically in supporting computational thinking and problem solving.
