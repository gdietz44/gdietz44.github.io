Hi! I'm Griffin Dietz.

I am a PhD candidate studying Computer Science at Stanford University in the Human-Computer Interaction Group. I am co-advised by James Landay in Computer Science and Hyowon Gweon in Psychology.

I'm interested in both understanding children's capacities for reasoning about technology and in building systems to support children's learning about that technology. My current work focuses on early childhood computer science education, specifically in supporting computational thinking and AI education.
